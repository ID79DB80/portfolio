Something about me

Watercolor / 2017

Farm in the Fall


Born and raised in Italy, after graduating in Theology, I moved to Peru, where I worked for an Italian NGO supporting the needy locals through community projects, education, and art workshops.

After completing my South American experience after 18 years, I moved to the U.S. in 2016, where I am now studying Digital Communication and pursuing an M.A. in Integrated Design at the University of Baltimore. To complement my career with more effective communication, I am also getting a B.A. in social psychology at SNHU.

In Baltimore, I still cultivated my earlier passion for fine arts by attending watercolor lessons under the direction of Frederic Briggs at the Schuler school.

My artistic journey, which started with drawing and fine arts, led me over time to master stained glass technics and now to explore Design and its digital applications.
